Product overview

One system for prescriptions, patients, and inventory.

RxFlow centralizes prescription workflow, patient and prescriber records, and drug inventory in a modern web application. It connects to external drug data feeds and transaction exchange simulations to demonstrate realistic interoperability while reducing implementation risk.

Business model

SaaS subscriptions with scalable add-ons.

RxFlow is sold per pharmacy location and/or per user seat, with tiered plans for advanced reporting, audit logs, and integrations. Approved vendors can advertise within the platform as an optional revenue stream.

Subscription SaaS

Recurring monthly or annual fees per location or seat.

Tiered add-ons

Premium analytics, extended data retention, and integrations.

Advertising
